Senior Customer Experience Manager

About Us:
We are a fast-growing SaaS company seeking an experienced Customer Experience Manager to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for driving customer satisfaction, retention, and growth through exceptional relationship management and strategic planning.

Key Responsibilities:
- Manage a portfolio of enterprise accounts with focus on customer satisfaction and retention
- Drive onboarding and implementation processes for new customers
- Monitor and analyze customer health metrics using tools like Salesforce and Gainsight
- Conduct quarterly business reviews and strategic planning sessions
- Develop and maintain relationships with key stakeholders
- Create and deliver customer training programs
- Identify and execute upsell opportunities
- Collaborate with product teams on customer feedback and feature requests

Requirements:
- 5+ years experience in Customer Success/Experience management
- Proven track record of managing enterprise SaaS accounts
- Strong proficiency with CRM tools (Salesforce, Gainsight)
- Experience with customer health monitoring and analytics
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Bachelor's degree in Business or related field
- Experience conducting QBRs and strategic planning sessions

Skills:
- Customer Success Management

- Stakeholder Management
- CRM Tools (Salesforce, Gainsight)
- Analytics and Reporting
- Customer Training
- Project Management
- Strategic Planning

Metrics We Care About:
